about

Angie Braun studied film directing at the Universidad del Cine (FUC). Two of his screenplays short film, “Noche de Búhos" and “Todas las Noches", were distinguished and selected to be directed by her. Captivated by the deep emotion of the still image, she began to delve into the picture. She studied Digital Photography at the Accademia di Art in Florence, Italy. In Buenos Aires she worked in the areas of production and lighting projects of other directors. She worked as a production assistant in “Herederos de una Venganza" and “El Puntero" in Pol-ka Producciones. Also as assistant director on History Channel and National Geographic. She studied acting at the School of Augustin Alezzo. In the last few years she decided to devote herself fully to photography working for clients such as Pia Carregal, La Cardeuse, Cool Hunter, Banco Supervielle, Aeropuertos Argentina 2000, Laurencio Adot, Audi, Paula and Agustina Ricci, among others…
